About Me

Hello, my name is Christopher Polizzi. I am a father, software engineer, truck driver, and nerd. Interesting combination, huh?

I am a big believer in the next generation. I have fostered and adopted six children. Raising children is one of my passions and I strive to be a good role model for them. I want to teach them to be kind, compassionate, and hardworking individuals. I want to give them the tools they need to succeed in life and be there for them whenever they need me.

I have worked in software development for about 15 plus years, specializing in web development. I love learning new technologies and sharing my knowledge with others.

When I lost my IT job in 2024, I had trouble finding another IT job. So I worked as an uber driver for a while. I was losing money each month and needed to figure out a way to pay my bills and support my family. Truck driving became a new opportunity that presented itself. I decided to get my commercial driver's license (CDL) and become a truck driver. There is something about driving a big rig that I find exhilarating. The open road, the freedom, and the sense of adventure. I really miss my kids when I'm on the road, but I know that I'm doing this to provide for them. I have been keeping an eye out for other opportinities to be able to be with my family more.

I really enjoy board games and video games. Some of my favorite board games are Pandemic, Catan, and Sushi Go. I even like dungeons and dragons. The thing I love about board games is that they bring people together. Also, I love the simplicity of the construction and the deep strategy. As for video games, I enjoy strategy games, puzzle games, RPGs, and Exploring games.I really like retro games as well. Some of my favorite video games are Final Fantasy 7, Subnautica, Tetris Attack, and Rock n' Roll Racing.
